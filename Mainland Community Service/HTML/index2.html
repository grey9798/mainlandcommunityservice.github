OUR FAQ

Freequently Ask Questions

01.What is Mainland Community Services Society?

We’re an incorporated community-based multi-services agency that delivers a continuum of programs and services in the Lower Mainland of British Columbia. We employ intersectional understanding and liberatory frameworks in empowering racialized individuals and communities in need by providing a voice, resources, and opportunities.

02.What do you do?

We’ve been supporting racialized communities in a variety of ways and measuring our success not by monetary size, but by more qualitative measurements such as the scale and effectiveness of our efforts.

03.What is ItStartsWithMe?

ItStartsWithMe social media campaign aims to widen the conversations on removing or preventing barriers to disability equity and accessibility in Canada through educational posts, and posts celebrating these achievements.

04.Where is Mainland Community Services Society located?

We are located in 308-515 West Pender Street, Vancouver, BC, V6B 1V5
